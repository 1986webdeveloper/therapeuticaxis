Our Therapists

Elly Gabdulkadyrova

Elly worked as a qualified speech pathologist with children for 8 years, before she undertook a Holistic Kinesiology course at the College of Complementary Medicine in 2006 and has since established a practice in Kinesiology. Elly is also a Master Reiki practitioner, and has practiced Reiki for last 10 years. Elly is passionate about her work and believes that everyone has the power to discover the way to create positive changes in their lives, living life through endless possibilities in harmony and joy within.
