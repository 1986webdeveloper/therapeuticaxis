ROLF RUHL

Along our path in life, many of us experience trying times. Challenges and difficulties may be greater than usual, or ways of dealing with them don't seem to work well anymore.

Often patterns of behaviour and coping strategies developed in childhood, in response to difficult circumstances, and which have become entrenched and unconscious, trouble us because they don't work in our adult world.

Rolf can assist you to bring to light, explore, address and change these old ways of relating to people and life situations. Rolf can help you with challenges including:

*Anxiety & depression
*Relationships
*Loss & grief
*Life transitions
*Trauma

B.A. B.D. Grad. Dip. Individual Psychotherapy & Relationship Therapy.
Clinical Member of Counsellors and Psychotherapist Association of NSW (CAPA)
Psychotherapy and Counselling Federation of Australia (PACFA) Reg. No. 20389.
