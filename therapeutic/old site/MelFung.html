Dr Mel Fung

Mel is trained to help people with stress, depression, addictions, eating issues, relationship problems and low self-esteem. She also has an interest in helping people with intellectual disabilities.

Mel is a Christian and attends an Anglican church on Sydney's North Shore. For clients who are also Christians, Mel respectfully uses Biblical teachings to guide and comfort.

Mel holds a Doctorate in Counselling Psychology from Macquarie University.
