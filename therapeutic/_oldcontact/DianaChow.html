We can better our lives and the lives of those around us through awareness, understanding, insight and love.” - Diana.

About Diana:
Diana has worked as a psychologist since 1997. She has worked in a range of settings including hospitals, universities, non-government organisations, schools, community health service and private practice. She has been a lecturer and supervisor for university post-graduate courses. She has also been involved with a number of population intervention projects targeting disadvantaged communities, children in need, and community education. Diana is dedicated to helping people heal, restore their intrinsic love, integrity, hope and passions in life.

Services offered:

  • Counselling and therapy for adults, couples, families, parents, adolescents and children.
  • Professional service with compassion and care.
  • Holistic approach to healing.
  • Integrative approach incorporating a range of evidence-based practices.
  • Medicare provider of clinical psychology services.
  • Medicare rebates.
  • Private health insurance rebates.

Qualifications and affiliations:

  • Masters Degree in Psychology (M.Ed, psychology)
  • Member of the Australian Psychological Society (APS)
  • Member of the APS College of Clinical Psychologists
  • Registered with the NSW Psychologist Registration Board
