Welcome

Therapeutic Axis has been created to ensure our clients have a professional, supportive experience.  We approach wellbeing holistically offering a range of modalities to support your needs.  Our therapists are qualified, insured, and offer the highest quality services in their field of expertise. Immediate appointments are generally available. If you are unsure of the type of therapist to best support you, please contact us and our Client Contact staff will be happy to chat with you.  We can consider all aspects from personal to financial during this quick and casual process.  Are you searching for supportive Counselling for yourself or your relationship?  Is your body telling you that a therapeutic and relaxing Massage is long overdue?  Perhaps you are searching for the holistic healing of Complementary Therapies for help with a specific condition or for general wellness.  
Let our Centre for Wellbeing help you on your path.

Counselling & Psychotherapy

Therapeutic Axis has counsellors, psychotherapists and psychologists who are qualified , dedicated and specialise in the treatment of Depression, Grief, Loss, Anger Management, Trauma, Addictions and Substance Abuse, Anxiety, Panic Attacks, Agoraphobia, Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD), Bipolar Disorder , Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD), Mood Disorder, Post Natal Depression and Personality Disorder. Relationship counselling, Positive Psychology, Buddhist Counselling, Hypnotherapy, Child & Adolescent Counselling, Dream Therapy, Mediation, Life-Coaching, Neuro-Linguistic- Programming (NLP), Somatic Psychotherapy and Psychological Testing can be provided by experienced practitioners. Medicare or private health fund rebates may be available.

Complementary Therapies

Complementary Therapies treat each individual holistically to help bring about balance and optimize wellness on a mental, emotional and physical level. All therapies available at Therapeutic Axis are delivered by suitably qualified and professionally associated practitioners. Acupuncture, Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), Chinese Herbal Medicine, Nutritional Counselling and Reconnective Healing®. Some Health Fund Rebates available.

How can we help you?

Do you know the type of therapist you need but don’t know the individual that best suits you? Or are you unsure what type of therapy can help? At Therapeutic Axis we have qualified staff members who can guide you through this process. Our Client Contact staff are informative, honest and supportive. We understand that most people are looking for help from someone who is professional and accountable, in an environment where they feel comfortable and respected.
