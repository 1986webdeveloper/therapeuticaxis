Hilary Steel

Services offered:

Individual counselling and debriefing for issues such as:

  • • creating a better work life balance
  • • finding ways of overcoming chronic tiredness, lack of motivation, anxiety or depression
  • • dealing with grief and loss
  • • critical incident debriefing
  • • improving relationships at home or at work

Individual therapy: ongoing therapy for those requiring more in depth work. Hilary is a qualified psychotherapist, with specific training in Self Psychology, a dynamic model encouraging individuals to experience self, not as static but as changing, with the capacity for growth and development over the whole life cycle

  • • develop stronger self esteem and resilience
  • • resolve long standing family of origin and relationship issues
  • • open up to the possibility of greater freedom and creativity in all aspects of your life

Executive mentoring and organisational change: professional guidance aimed at maximising performance and improving communications in the workplace. Hilary has extensive experience as a manager and organisational consultant and works easily at all organizational levels.

  • • executive mentoring and coaching
  • • making decisions and taking action on career changes
  • • team building and conflict resolution
  • • strategy workshops

Qualifications: BA (Syd) MBA (NSW) Diploma of Adult Psychotherapy (ANZAP)
